stippled pepes

between 2022-2023, i created a series of stipple-based generative art pieces inspired by the rare pepe movement — the original crypto art community that launched on bitcoin's counterparty protocol in 2016.

these were issued as pepe cards on counterparty under the artist name STIPPLE.

below are curated composite layouts of the series. click any image to view it larger.
